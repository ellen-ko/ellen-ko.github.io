For this assignment, I programmed my Arduino to play Morse code using the buzzer. When the alligator clip circuit on pin A9 is open, the buzzer spells out “ELLEN,” and when the clip is closed, it switches to “SOS.” I set up one base time unit so that all the Morse patterns—dots, dashes, and pauses stay proportional no matter how fast or slow I make it. Getting the spacing right between letters and words took a few tries and I learned that the delay after each symbol already counts as part of the total letter or word gap. Here’s my board:
