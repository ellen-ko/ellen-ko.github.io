For this assignment, I programmed my Arduino to play the opening of “Daisy Bell (Bicycle Built for Two)” when the slide switch is turned on. The buzzer is connected to pin A3 and the switch to pin 6, using so that flipping the switch on triggers the melody. When the switch is off, the buzzer stays silent.
I ran into an interesting bug where my A3 notes didn’t play—turns out Arduino already uses A3 as an analog pin name! Renaming the variable to just A fixed it. Here's my board:
